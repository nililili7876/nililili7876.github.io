统计学习2_Classification

CM2_Basic methods for Classification

问题描述：数据：Target variable: Y ∈ {1, . . . , K}, n observations (target, features) $(X^1 , . . . X^p ) ∈ X$, 目标: define classification rules to predict Y from X.

MAP rule:

  • Maximum a posteriori[后验概率，就是条件概率]
  • $k(x)=argmax_{k=1..K}P(Y=k|X=x)$==>assigning a point x to the group for which the posterior probability is the greatest.
  • The MAP rule is a Bayes rule[?] for the 0-1 loss

1.Generative Models

1.1 基本定义

定义回顾

  • Based on the conditional distribution (X|Y) distribution and the Bayes Theorem
  • 基于联合分布P(X,Y)
  • Gaussians, Naive Bayes, Linear / Quadratic，Discriminant Analysis, Hidden Markov Models (HMM), Bayesian，networks, Markov random fields

X和Y

  • Outcome variable：Y ∈ Y = {1, . . . , K}，Y follows a discrete distribution$\sum^K_{k=1}π_k δ_{y=k}$ on {1,…,K}, $π_k = P(Y = k)$ : a priori probabilities.[先验概率，非条件概率]
  • Vector of features：X = (X1, . . . , Xj, . . . , Xp) ∈ X，The Xj’s are continuous or categorical variables，The conditional distribution of (X|Y = k) admits a density fk with respect to a ref. measure µ on X==[允许密度fk相对于参考点μ在X上测量?]==

MAP rule for generative models

  • $\pi_k$[先验概率]和$P(X = x| Y=k)$[后验概率]都是未知的
  • 我们在参数化设置中推断这些数量==>极大似然估计

1.2 Naive Bayes[朴素贝叶斯]

Naive Bayes Assumption: $X_j$与Y独立条件分布，$P(X=x|Y=k) = \prod_{j=1}^pP(X_j=x_j|Y=k)$，p是X的特征数

Parametric assumptions and Maximum Likelihood Estimation

  • observations $(X_i,Y_i)$是独立的
  • 对于任意的(j,k),假定$(X_j|Y=k)$的分布在一个如下的parametric model中
    • 允许密度$x_j —> f_{j,k}(,x_j)$
    • the vector of parameters $η_{j,k}$ lies in a parameter space $E_{j,k}$ .

Example: Spam detection

2. Discriminative methods

2.1 Logistic Regression

判别式方法：从(Y|X)分布中定义模型

假设：$y \in {-1和1的集合}$

模型：$ P(Y=1|X=x) = \sigma(+b) $，我们把w叫做权重weights, b叫做截矩intercept，然后$\sigma$是激活函数

  • 激活函数是为了把R map到[0,1]，R是实数
  • 模型也可以选择为$ P(Y=1|X=x) = F(+b) $，F是高斯分布

Inference：

  • 条件分布的极大似然估计：$\prod_{j=1}^pP(X_j=x_j|Y=k)$可以得到
  • 是个凸优化问题

2.2 Nearest neighbors

KNN分类：y(x) = Majority vote on Y over the K-NN of x in the sample.

It works in any metric space[可度量的] but … you need to choose the metric

K的选择==>可以用交叉熵

2.3 Other methods & Conclusion

Other methods:

  • SVMs and kernel methods
  • Random Forests
  • Boosting
  • Neural networks and Deep Learning

Sum up :

  • Logistic regression is very popular for classification, especially when K = 2. In particular, logistic regression with binary variables and Lasso penalization[正则化的一个例子，通过增加额外参数来解决过拟合问题，从而减少模型的参数、限制复杂度] can be very efficient in practice.
  • LDA (LQA)[????] is useful when the classes are well separated, and Gaussian assumptions are reasonable.
  • Naive Bayes is useful when p is very large.
  • K-NN works in general metric spaces.
  • 用于分类的其他的判别式模型：SVMs and kernel methods、Random Forests、Boosting、Neural networks and Deep Learning

3. Multiclass and multilabel classification

  1. Multiclass
    • a classification task with more than two classes (e.g. several
      animals)==>非二元的分类
    • each sample is assigned to one and only one label: a fruit can be either an apple or a pear but not both at the same time.==>每个样本只有一个标签
  2. Multilabel
    • each sample is assigned to a set of target labels.
    • label = properties that are not mutually exclusive[互不排斥的属性], e.g. (topics of a collection of books)

如何用二元分类器解决多标签多类别的分类问题？==> One Versus All strategy

  • 对于每个class，只有是和不是这两种
  • Interpretability[可解释性] : since each class is represented by one and only one classifier, it is possible to gain knowledge about the class by inspecting its corresponding classifier.
  • The final decision corresponds to the classifier with the highest score

4. 一些误差

4.1 Confusion Matrix

False Positive (FP) rate： The fraction of negative examples that are classified as positive.==>被归类为正面的负面例子的比例。

False Negative (FN) rate：The fraction of positive examples that are classified as negative.

同理有True Positive (TP) and True Negative (TN)

4.2 Varying the threshold (two classes problem)

对于二分类的问题，MAP规则是$y(x)=1 if P(Y=1|X=x)>1/2$然后我们可以改变这个阈值1/2,然后有个这样子的图像==>so??????

4.3 ROC Curve (Receiver Operating Characteristic)

曲线上的点对应$(FP_n,TP_n)$,n是新的阈值

曲线一定是不减的[x+y=1]

(0,1)点对应zero error的分类规则

AUC score is the Area Under the ROC Curve.

4.4 Unbalanced classes in classification

就是一个东西可能即是这个class又是那个class，然后在这种情况下，classification rules tend to predict only the majority class : accuracy is not enough.

  • Rebalance the metric. Empirical risk with rebalanced loss

  • oversampling methods : creates copy data or create synthetic samples (SMOTE) from the minor class[小类]
